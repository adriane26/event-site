Microsoft interop events are free. Microsoft interoperability events are focused on building knowledge, not selling tickets. The fundamentals of Microsoft Open Specifications and interoperability are based on providing technical training and documentation free of charge.

We are aligned with the open specifications' values, because we believe you should have access to the latest interoperability knowledge and product support for free. It’s your event, our treat—we'll even cover lunch!
